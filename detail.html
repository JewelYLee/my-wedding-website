The Big Day

When

Saturday, April 26, 2014

Where

The reception will be held at Ziga Zaga in Grand Hyatt, a restaurant featuring authentic Italian cuisine plus American and Mexican specialities. Cocktail hour will start around 4:45ish. Dinner and the epic dance party will start shortly after.

The address of Grand Hyatt Hotel is 2, SongShou Road, Taipei, Taiwan, 11051. Once you entered the main entrance, the escalator on you left will take you directly to Ziga Zaga.

Directions

If you drive: Head east, drive on ZhongXiao E. Rd towards MRT Taipei City Hall station. Take a right onto SongRen Rd and then take the right onto the busy SongShou Rd. From there, you will see Grand Hyatt Hotel on the left shortly after you drive pass Vietshow and Exhibition Hall 3. The Grand Hyatt parking lot will be immediately on your right when you turn left onto Shif Rd.

If you take MRT: Get off at Taipei City Hall station and take Exit 2. Follow the signs towards Taipei 101, Grand Hyatt Hotel is right next to it. It's a 8 minutes walk from the exit to Grand Hyatt.

Bring the map that was included with your invitation with you! -- It will help.
